<ol>
<li>For Chronic back/neck pain:</li>
<ul>
<li>Yoga
<li>Always use a chair that's ergonomically designed for giving proper support and is according to your height and weight. You can also use a small pillow for lumbar support.</li>
<li>Keep your back aligned with the back of the office chair, as this will help in avoiding slouching or leaning forward.</li>
<li>When sitting, always keep your shoulders straight and squared, your head upright, and your neck, back, and heels should be in alignment.</li>
</ul>
<li>For Kyphotic lower back:</li>
<ul><li>When used along with bracing, physical therapy can be helpful to build strength, flexibility, and increase range of motion. Emphasis is on increasing the flexibility of the back and hamstring muscles, increasing back strength, and improving posture.</li>
</ul>
<li>For Cardiovascular and heart diseases:</li>
<ul>
<li>Know your blood pressure and keep it under control.</li>
<li>Exercise regularly.</li>
<li>Don't smoke.</li>
<li>Get tested for diabetes and if you have it, keep it under control.</li>
<li>Know your cholesterol and triglyceride levels and keep them under control.</li>
<li>Eat a lot of fruits and vegetables.</li>
<li>Maintain a healthy weight.</li>
</ul>
</ol>
