<ol>
<li>For Carotid Atherosclerosis:</li>
<ul>
<li>Heart-Healthy Eating:Adopt heart-healthy eating habits, which include eating different fruits and vegetables (including beans and peas), whole grains, lean meats, poultry without skin, seafood, and fat-free or low-fat milk and dairy products. A heart-healthy diet is low in sodium, added sugar, solid fats, and refined grains.</li>
<li>Physical activity</li>
<li>Quit smoking</li>
<li>Keep obesity in check</li>
</ul>
<li>For Varicose Veins:</li>
<ul>
<li>Exercising</li>
<li>Watching your weight</li>
<li>Eating a high-fiber, low-salt diet</li>
<li>Avoiding high heels and tight hosiery</li>
<li>Elevating your legs</li>
<li>Changing your sitting or standing position regularly</li>
<li>Don't sit with your legs crossed.</li>
</ul>
<li>For flat/ protruding chest(pectus carinatum/pigeon chest):</li>
<ul>
<li>Use of external orthotic bracing</li>
<li>Proper symmetric body building</li>
<li>The Hip Lift Exercises</li>
<li>Toe Tapping Exercises</li>
<li>Squatting & Kickback</li>
</ul>
</ol>
